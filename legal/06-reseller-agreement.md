## 1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and hold a valid Saw Yun account to participate. We may approve, decline, suspend, or remove any reseller from the program at our discretion, including for the reasons described in Section 7.

## 2. How the Program Works

- We provide you with a unique referral link and/or code to share with prospective customers.
- Referred customers who sign up using your link/code may be eligible for a referral trial (currently 45 days; subject to change).
- When a referred customer converts to and maintains a paid subscription, you may earn a commission as described in Section 3.
- Referral attribution, trial terms, and eligible products may change at our discretion; we will provide reasonable notice of material changes.

## 3. Commissions

- **Commission rate:** 10% of the referred customer's payment. This rate applies for now and may be revised by Saw Yun LLC at any time for future referrals.
- **Commission duration:** Commissions are earned once when a referred customer first activates a paid subscription, and again if that customer later upgrades to a higher-tier plan (calculated on the upgrade payment). Commissions are not currently earned on ordinary renewals at the same plan; Saw Yun LLC may introduce renewal-based or other recurring commission structures in the future, in which case this section will be updated accordingly.
- **Eligible transactions:** Commissions apply only to genuine, active paying subscriptions resulting directly from your referral — not to your own account, accounts you control, or fraudulent/duplicate signups.
- Commission rates, structures, and eligible products may be updated by us from time to time; changes apply prospectively to new referrals unless we state otherwise.

## 4. Wallet & Payouts

- Earned commissions accrue to a reseller wallet balance associated with your account, denominated in Myanmar Kyats (MMK).
- **Minimum payout threshold:** 50,000 MMK (Myanmar Kyats). Balances below this amount remain in your wallet and roll over until the threshold is met. This threshold may be updated by Saw Yun LLC from time to time.
- **Payout schedule:** On request. Once your wallet balance meets or exceeds the minimum payout threshold, you may request a payout; we do not process payouts automatically on a fixed schedule.
- **Payout methods** follow our standard [Pricing and Payments terms](./01-terms-of-service.md#14-pricing-and-payments) and may include bank transfer, mobile payments, digital wallets, or other approved methods. Manual verification may be required before a payout is released.
- You are responsible for providing accurate payout information. We are not responsible for payouts sent to incorrect details you provided.

## 5. Clawbacks & Reversals

We may reverse, deduct, or claw back a commission from your wallet balance (including from future earnings, if the balance is insufficient) if:

- The referred customer receives a refund, cancels within an initial period, or has their subscription terminated for breach of the Terms of Service;
- The referral is later determined to be fraudulent, self-referred, or otherwise ineligible under Section 3; or
- The commission was credited in error.
