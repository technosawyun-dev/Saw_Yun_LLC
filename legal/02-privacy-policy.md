# Privacy Policy

**Saw Yun LLC**
Effective Date: July 20, 2026 (tentative — subject to change before launch)
Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Saw Yun LLC ("Saw Yun," "we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, across all of our current and future products and services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Services.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Information required to provide the Services:**

- Name
- Email address
- Business name
- Device ID
- IP address
- Browser information
- Operating system
- Crash logs
- Product usage analytics
- License keys
- Payment references
- Customer database records you enter into the Services (e.g., your own customers' data, if you use a product to manage them)

**Optional information, depending on how you use the Services:**

- Phone number
- Business address
- Employee information
- Uploaded files
- Inventory data

We collect this information directly from you, automatically through your use of the Services (e.g., device and usage data), and, where applicable, from your staff or team members you invite to your account.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account and license;
- Provide, operate, and maintain the Services;
- Process payments and verify transactions;
- Activate and validate software licenses;
- Send administrative communications, including receipts, password resets, and service notices;
- Provide customer support;
- Monitor, diagnose, and improve the Services (including through crash logs and usage analytics);
- Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues; and
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

- **Service providers ("sub-processors")** who help us operate the Services. Currently this includes:
  - Cloudflare (including Cloudflare Tunnel and Cloudflare R2, used for infrastructure, backups, and content delivery)
  - Mailtrap (used for transactional email, such as password resets and receipts)
  - Our self-hosted VPS provider (used for hosting the Services and databases)
- **Future service providers** we may add as we grow, including payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Wise), analytics providers, and AI providers. We will update this Policy as these are adopted.
- **Legal and safety reasons**, where required to comply with law, respond to legal process, or protect the rights, property, or safety of Saw Yun LLC, our customers, or others.
- **Business transfers**, if Saw Yun LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We require our service providers to protect your information consistently with this Policy.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain information according to the following general schedule, unless a longer period is required by law:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account information | Until deletion request, plus 90 days |
| Backups | 90 days |
| Crash logs | 12 months |
| Billing records | 7 years |
| License records | While the license is active, plus a reasonable retention period afterward |

## 5. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support@sawyuntech.com or legal@sawyuntech.com. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable time, subject to the retention schedule above. We may retain certain information after a deletion request where necessary for:

- Legal obligations;
- Accounting and tax requirements;
- Fraud prevention;
- Security; and
- License verification.

Backups containing your data will expire naturally according to our backup retention schedule rather than being individually purged.

## 6. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including JWT-based authentication, infrastructure via Cloudflare (including Cloudflare Tunnel for secure connectivity), and self-hosted servers running PostgreSQL and Redis. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Saw Yun LLC serves individuals, businesses, and organizations worldwide. Because our infrastructure and service providers may operate in the United States and other countries, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. As our international customer base grows, we are working toward aligning our practices with applicable frameworks such as the GDPR (for users in the EU/EEA) and CCPA/CPRA (for California residents).

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at legal@sawyuntech.com. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under 18 years old, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact us at legal@sawyuntech.com and we will take appropriate action.

## 10. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Our websites and applications may use cookies or similar technologies necessary for authentication, session management, and basic analytics. We do not currently use cookies for third-party advertising.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or notice within the Services. Your continued use of the Services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
